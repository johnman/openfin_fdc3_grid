This is a basic example of two OpenFin apps using the News Intent

One app (the publisher) lists instruments in a grid and uses a row selection to raise an fdc3 news intent asking for information for that instrument:

The other app listens to the fdc3 News Intent and adds instrument selections to a grid to indicate this is what someone wants to see news about.
